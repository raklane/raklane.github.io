About Me

Hello! I'm Rakesh — a Software Developer in Test with + years of experience building test automation frameworks for some of the most recognizable products on the internet. I got my start automating regulatory reporting at Deutsche Bank, and have since led quality initiatives at HSBC, CEMEX, T-Mobile, Charter Communications, and St. Jude Hospital.

Today, at Snap Inc., I partner with Engineering, Product, Design, and Trust & Safety to drive release readiness for Snapchat — from planning through global launch. I'm especially focused on AI-enabled QA: using agents, Claude Code, and Cursor to expedite test generation, triage bugs, and standardize test plans from specification documents.

I believe great quality engineering is less about finding bugs and more about preventing them — through smart tooling, great process, and sharp judgment on where risk lives.
